Corned Beedf Nachos

Whether you’re watching a big sports match or having game night with friends, a plate of nachos is a surefire way to get people even more pumped! There’s a reason it remains to be one of the most popular appetizers across the world. There are some dishes that you’re simply willing to get your hands…

  • Preparation Time: 5 mins
  • Cooking Time: 20 mins
  • Total Duration: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 260 grams CDO Home Style Corned Beef in Pasta Sauce
  • 16 oz. tortilla chips
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 ½ teaspoons taco seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil

Topping Ingredients

  • 120 grams cheese dip
  • ½ cup green onions chopped
  • ½ cup black olives pitted and sliced
  • 1 tomato seeds removed and diced
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese shredded

Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce by heating the cooking in a pan. Sauté onion until it softens. Add CDO Home Style Corned Beef in Pasta Sauce. Cook for 2 minutes. Add taco seasoning. Stir and cook for 2 to 3 minutes. Let it cool down.
  2. Arrange tortilla chips on a wide plate. Drizzle cheese dip all over and top with meat sauce.
  3. Spread the remaining topping ingredients over the meat sauce.
  4. Serve. Share and enjoy!

Notes

  1. Make sure you get a tortilla large enough to pile onto; you’ll definitely need it for this nachos recipe’s perfect bite!
  2. Add onions, olives, tomatoes, and lots of cheese to your nacho chip. Of course, you can’t forget the quintessential CDO Home Style Corned Beef as well.
  3. It may be quite a big bite, but it’s definitely worth it. All the flavors that marry in your mouth make for dynamic textures and tastes.
  4. That yumminess is surely going to make you want even more!
